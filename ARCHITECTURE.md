# ARCHITECTURE.md

## Why Enforcement Exists

The Kemet Backend prioritizes enforcement over conventions to ensure consistency, reliability, and clarity across all aspects of the system. Relying on strict enforcement reduces ambiguity, prevents accidental deviations, and enables both humans and AI to collaborate effectively without misunderstandings. This approach minimizes the risk of errors and streamlines onboarding, maintenance, and automation.

## Human Responsibilities

Humans are responsible for making key decisions, setting project direction, and defining the rules that govern the system. They provide context, resolve ambiguities, and document decisions that cannot be automated. Humans are also accountable for reviewing and approving changes that impact business logic, security, or project goals.

## AI Responsibilities

AI is responsible for executing tasks within the boundaries set by human-defined rules and documentation. This includes automating repetitive work, enforcing standards, and ensuring compliance with project requirements. AI assists by interpreting and applying enforcement mechanisms, but does not override or reinterpret human decisions.
